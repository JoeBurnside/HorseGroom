Welcome to the Horse and Groom in Basford

We are a traditional local public house offering a range of top quality cask conditioned beers from around the country. We pride ourselves on offering our customers unrivaled quality in fine ales, served by our friendly and efficient staff. The Horse and Groom is well known for the quality of the real ale served through one of the nine regularly rotated hand pumps. We have a permanent pump for differing Milds, a once popular drink seeing a resurgence and a wide selection of local brews as we are keen followers of the CAMRA LocAle scheme.
